Why This Exists

Many skating resources focus on competition or paid lessons. Skate Elements removes barriers and emphasizes skill-building, creativity, and inclusivity.

Inclusive

Skaters of all ages, backgrounds, and levels are welcomed.

Accessible

Learn foundational skills without expensive lessons.

Creative

Encourages personal expression over perfection.

Basic Skating Elements

These elements give you the tools to skate creatively while building confidence and control.

Edges

Inside and outside edges improve balance, flow, and control.

Turns & Steps

Three-turns, mohawks, and crossovers let skaters connect moves and explore flow.

Spins

Upright, sit, and camel spins teach balance and body awareness.

Jumps

Waltz jumps, toe loops, and more give expressive opportunities.

Transitions

Link elements together to create seamless, expressive sequences.

Choreography

Blend skills with music and emotion to tell your own story on ice.

Moments of Expression

Snapshots of creativity and artistry on the ice.

A Welcoming Community

By moving focus away from rankings and individual tracking, we create space for creativity, growth, and belonging.
